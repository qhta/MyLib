WritingSystemKind Enumeration

Namespace: Qhta.Unicode.Models
Assembly: Qhta.UnicodeBuild (in Qhta.UnicodeBuild.dll)
Specifies the kind of writing system used to represent language or symbols.

Remarks
 
This enumeration categorizes various writing systems based on their structural and functional characteristics. It includes systems such as alphabets, abjads, syllabaries, and more specialized forms like musical notation and stenography.
