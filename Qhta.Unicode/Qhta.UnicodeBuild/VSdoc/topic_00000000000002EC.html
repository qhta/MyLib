RowResizer Class

Namespace: Qhta.SF.Tools
Assembly: Qhta.SF.Tools (in Qhta.SF.Tools.dll)
A Thumb control that allows resizing of rows in a SfDataGrid. It is typically used at the bottom of the row header of a grid to adjust the height of rows. This class was written on the basis of the GridSplitter class, but adapted for rows.
